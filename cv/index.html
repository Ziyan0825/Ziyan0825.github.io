    M.Sc. Dissertation · University College London

    Preparation of Graphene Composite as Barrier Coating for OTFT/OLED Active Matrix Displays

    Institution
    University College London
    Supervisor
    Prof. Kwang-Leong Choy and Dr. Mingqing Wang
    Research Objective

    To obtain graphene / epoxy resin nanocomposites with enhanced gas-barrier properties, and compare the enhancement and other material properties of graphene oxide (GO) and reduced graphene oxide (rGO).

    Experiments
    • Used Hummer's method for oxidizing graphite to GO.
    • Used solution mixing to prepare GO and rGO / epoxy resin nanocomposites, followed by spin coating for film preparation.
    • Characterized materials using UV, water contact angle, SEM, XRD, profiler, FTIR, and AFM methods.
    Key Results

    B.Eng. Dissertation · University of Nottingham Ningbo China

    Large Eddy Simulation of Bubble Column Two-Phase Bubbly Flow with Considerations of Different Interfacial Forces

    Institution
    University of Nottingham Ningbo China
    Supervisor
    Prof. Xiaogang Yang and Dr. Shanshan Long
    Research Objective

    To investigate the influence of interfacial forces, including drag force, lift force, and virtual mass force, under LES on numerical simulation of bubble column two-phase bubbly flow.

    Experiments
    • Built the simulation based on the previous experimental system of Sanyal, using large eddy simulation as the turbulence model.
    • Compared three numerical cases with experimental data to investigate transient radial gas holdup and axial liquid flow rate.
    • Used Gambit 2.4.6 for grid generation and CFX 18.0 for numerical simulation.
